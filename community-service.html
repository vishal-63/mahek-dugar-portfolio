Project Gyanshaala - Empowering Through Education
  • Mission:

To recognize the right to education by providing appropriate learning environments, enhancing educational experiences and creating positive transformations for the children of construction workers.

  • Journey of Project Gyaanshala:
  • Our Approach:

In pursuit of the mission, as a team we started with a fundraiser and embarked on an incredible journey right from building a school premise to refurbishing it with attractive wall paintings, to developing a curriculum to providing educational kits for the children of the construction workers. We taught them English and Hindi languages, basic Mathematics, Indian Geography, and Physics. To ensure effective learning, we employed a combination of conventional and unconventional teaching methods, encouraging better memory retention and instilling a lifelong passion for knowledge.

  • Impact and Achievements:

Aligning with the United Nation SDGl 4: Quality Education, we could touch the lives of 25 children by opening their doors to better education. We aspire to contribute to a future where every child has access to a high-quality education.

Feets on Street- A Green Carnival
  • Aim of the Project:

    To sensitize the community about the importance of a green and sustainable world by engaging them through some games and activities.

  • Experience:

    As a team, we successfully engaged people of all age groups, from school kids to college students and even government officials, including the Mayor of the city. Through games like Sack Race, Hop Scotch, Tic Tac Toe, and Spin the Wheel, we brought back the joy of long-forgotten games, using eco-friendly materials such as jute bags, stones, and leaves. The carnival served as a platform to raise awareness about saving the environment, which is a pressing global concern.
