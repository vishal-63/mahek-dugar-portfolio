Education for Transformation
Project Gyaanshala

At Project Gyanshaala, we recognize the right to education and dedicate ourselves to providing learning opportunities and creating positive transformations. With a focus on the children of construction workers in the Shela region of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, we aim to create appropriate learning environments and enhance educational experiences.

Our Approach
In pursuit of our mission, starting with a fundraiser we embarked on an incredible journey right from building a school premise to refurbishing it with attractive wall paintings, to developing a curriculum encompassing subjects and basic skills for the children of the construction workers. We taught them English and Hindi languages, basic Mathematics, Indian Geography, and Physics. To ensure effective learning, we employed a combination of conventional and unconventional teaching methods, encouraging better memory retention and instilling a lifelong passion for knowledge.

Impact and Achievements: Enriching Lives through Education
By unleashing the potential through quality education, we empowered around 25 children to become informed and active members of society. We observed tremendous personal growth and learning outcomes as we took on challenges, developed new skills, and fostered collaboration. Aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4: Quality Education, we aspire to contribute to a future where every child has access to a high-quality education.

Counter Speech Fellowship
Young Leader for Active Citizenship (YLAC)

Being selected for this 8 week Fellowship in collaboration with Instagram was incredible. Through our handle ergo_the _chnage, we, as a team created meaningful content to educate and empower the larger community on some important themes like body positivity, digital safety, teenage wellbeing and diversity, equity, inclusion(DEI).
Every week we created innovative posts to create awareness and enable the larger community with the appropriate knowledge to deal with various issues listed above. Our posts included thought provoking write ups, important statistics, interesting reels, impactful posters, soul touching music, and some heart rending conversations which could inspire positive changes in the society.
This fellowship helped me grow as a leader and a change-maker. It inspired me to continue using my voice, creativity, and knowledge to address social issues and make a positive impact in the world. I am grateful for this transformative experience and excited to carry forward the lessons learned as I strive to create a better and more inclusive world.

A Green Carnival
Feets On Street

Aim of the project To sensitize the community about the importance of a green and sustainable world by engaging them through some games and activities.

Experience
As a team, we successfully engaged people of all age groups, from school kids to college students and even government officials, including the Mayor of the city. Through games like Sack Race, Hop Scotch, Tic Tac Toe, and Spin the Wheel, we brought back the joy of long-forgotten games, using eco-friendly materials such as jute bags, stones, and leaves. Extensive planning was crucial before the carnival to ensure we met all the requirements for our games.

Challenges
One of the challenges we faced was effectively explaining to young school children the importance of saving the environment and the practical ways to achieve it. However, through creativity and patience, we managed to convey this vital message.

Outcome
Collaborating with new team members, I learned the importance of effective teamwork and how to work harmoniously towards a shared goal.

Impact
The carnival served as a platform to raise awareness about saving the environment, a pressing global concern. It was a gratifying experience to contribute towards this essential cause and inspire others to take action.
