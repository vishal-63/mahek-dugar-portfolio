Digital Quest
Empowering Communities through Digital Inclusion
"Through digital inclusion, we empower individuals to turn their dreams into realities and build a better tomorrow."
Mission

To foster digital inclusion and sustainability, ensuring that individuals from all walks of life have equal access to the opportunities offered by the digital world.

Journey at Digital Quest

My journey with Digital Quest has been a remarkable one, driven by the passion to bridge the digital divide and empower communities through digital inclusion.

Key Initiatives at Digital Quest

1st Tournament - October 2022 - Targeting Open Category The first step towards achieving our mission was organizing the charity badminton tournament. With an overwhelming response from 40 enthusiastic participants in the Open Category at the O7 Club in Ahmedabad, exceeding our expectations we raised $500.

2nd Tournament - January 2023 - Empowering Young Athletes Building on the success of the first tournament, we organized the second event exclusively for participants aged 9 to 17 at the Ahmedabad Youth Badminton Academy. This time, 75 young athletes showcased their skills, captivating everyone with exhilarating rallies. The event raised $700 and received positive feedback from participants and their parents.

My Experience

It was an enthralling experience right from meetings with the club's managers to designing an eye-catching flyer, to creating Google forms to marketing the event on social media to handling participant inquiry and recruiting professional referees. During the tournaments we witnessed some of the most remarkable performances from participants, including state champions. The prize distribution ceremony was an honorable moment as the managing director of the club presented the awards. At the end, I see myself equipped with skills like teamwork, coordination, leadership, and also understand the significance of recognition for hard work.

E-Waste Drives
Recognizing the importance of digital literacy and sustainability, we also organized e-waste drives in local communities and industrial areas. Through these drives, we aimed to raise awareness about responsible e-waste disposal. Approximately 80 kg of e-waste was collected which was sent to ECS, Environment Pvt. Ltd, a leading company in the state for e- waste recycling. Proceeds of around $100 were received and contributed to our cause.

The Outcome

First Computer Center at Nadiad in Collaboration with Tresna Foundation Through our initiatives and fundraisers, we established a thriving computer center in a village at Nadiad, in collaboration with Tresna Foundation, Ahmedabad. We donated 5 computer sets to the center where 50 students, mostly first-generation learners aged 13 to 16, currently study. Three skilled trainers ensure a comprehensive learning experience
The students' feedback at the center has been immensely rewarding. Their happiness and gratitude reinforce our belief in the importance of digital inclusion. Equipping these students with digital skills opens better job opportunities, supports their families, and contributes to society.
