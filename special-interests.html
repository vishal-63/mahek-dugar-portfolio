My Diverse Passions

Step into my world of diverse passions, where I find joy and fulfillment in pursuing various hobbies that shape my identity.

Playing Piano

For the past 8 years, the piano has been my loyal companion, opening up a world of melodies and emotions. Achieving Merit positions up to Level 5 in the Trinity exams has given credibility to my dedication and love for this instrument. The piano has not only enriched my life with its harmonious notes but has also taught me discipline and perseverance, guiding me through both challenging pieces and soulful compositions.

Reading Books
Previous Next

In this digital age, I find solace in turning pages. From Shakespeare's "Merchant of Venice" to Rowling's "Harry Potter" series, to Hoovers “It Ends With Us”, I've immersed myself in captivating tales and profound life lessons. As my love for literature grew, so did my passion for philosophy.
Joining a book club connected me with like-minded individuals, where we engaged in passionate discussions, shared unique perspectives, and explored diverse writing styles. It became a space for growth, fun, and knowledge exchange.
If you find me alone in a room, there will be a book in my hands—an extension of who I am. Eagerly anticipating reading "Pride and Prejudice" and "A Tale of Two Cities," I'm ready for another literary adventure.

Dancing & Film-Making

Dancing
Dancing has always been my avenue for celebration, self-expression, and pure happiness. Whether it's a carefully choreographed routine or an impromptu dance-off with friends it helps me instill confidence, and freeing my spirit. It's a constant reminder that life is meant to be embraced with grace.

Movies and Film making
Movies are not just a form of entertainment; they are a gateway to imagination. I am drawn to the power of cinema, the way it transports us to different worlds and emotions. From watching dramas to thrillers, each film has left an indelible mark on my creativity. Love for movies made me participate in a 3-day filmmaking workshop where I delved into the art of storytelling and had the opportunity to create a short film titled "Justice in Jeopardy." This immersive experience taught me the importance of collaboration, storytelling, and how the art of filmmaking comes to life on the screen.
