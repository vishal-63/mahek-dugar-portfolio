Embracing the Athlete Within

For me, embracing the athlete within means diving headfirst into thrilling experiences of sports and adventures, where I discover my inner strength, passion, and embrace challenges with a sportsman spirit.

Sports and Achievements

One of my fondest memories is winning the sack race at the age of 8. This moment motivated me to actively pursue sports and I went on exploring various track and field events along with indoor games like badminton.Through years of practice and training, I could ace sprints, relay, hurdles and long jumps by taking away the gold in each of them consecutively for three years from 2018 to 2020. The experience of crossing the finishing line remains unparalleled.
Being the captain of my school house team for Kho-kho, Kabaddi,and Football has helped me understand how leadership, teamwork and strategies can make you embrace challenges and win games. I have always enjoyed playing badminton tournaments for years and even organized one as part of my passion project, raising funds for digital literacy. I strongly believe that sports bring out the best in people, teaches competitiveness, and instills a go-getter attitude.

From the rushing rapids across rivers to rock climbing, to sky cycling to zip lining and sandwave boarding, each experience has encouraged me to push my limits and test my strength and determination.
