About

Hi! Welcome to my page. Here is the journey of my experiences that have shaped me into who I am today.

Following Steve Jobs' philosophy, I've always been passionate about pursuing my interests rather than conforming to prescribed courses. After having studied ICSE till Grade 10, I moved to the IB curriculum where I could explore and pursue my interests in the disciplines of economics, business, math, and physics. I braved myself further by participating in Economics and English Olympiads. Additionally, at Early Steps Academy, I completed a Seven Level Global Certification Programme, which opened my doors to the core concepts of Digital Technology, Entrepreneurship, Personal Finance and Crypto that fueled my ambition to explore these areas further. Furthermore, through a project based approach at a summer camp on Python and AI at IIT Delhi, I took my first step towards understanding analytical, logical and algorithmic thinking which I believe is pertinent to thrive in the tech-driven world of tomorrow.

Digital Quest- my passion project, is an initiative to enable digital inclusion where it is deprived. Through a series of Charity Badminton Tournaments and E- Waste Drives in the city, I have been able to take some baby steps in setting up a Computer Center for young children in the village of Nadiad (Gujarat) in collaboration with Tresna Foundation, Ahmedabad. Constant efforts are underway to sustain the functioning of this center to help these children excel their digital skills.

Being passionate about working in areas focusing digital literacy, teenage well-being, and body positivity, I was chosen for the Counter Speech Fellowship program of the Young Leaders for Active Citizenship (YLAC) in collaboration with Instagram, where I had the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to these causes along with a likeminded cohort.

Leading Project Gyaanshala, I worked with my friends to build and refurbish a school at a site for the children of the construction workers and also helped them build foundations of basic education. This experience instilled in me a deep sense of empathy and the desire to give back to society.

Music is my absolute passion, and I've been playing the piano for 8 years now. I've been able to secure Merit positions at the prestigious Trinity exams up to Level 5! I'm also an avid reader and movie lover - there's nothing like losing yourself in a good book or immersing yourself in a great film.

As I look ahead, my aspirations lie in diving deeper into studying business analytics and understanding how technology and data can help me achieve personal, professional, and societal goals. If any of this resonates with you, I'd love to connect and share more about our common interests and passions. Thanks for visiting!
