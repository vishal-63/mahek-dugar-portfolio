Community Contribution
Check My Community Contribution

Contributing and giving back to the community has always given me a sense of purpose and direction. It helps me connect to real-life problems, develop empathy, and understanding, and become aware citizens.
With my initiative, Project Gyaanshala, we raised funds for the building of a school and I was actively involved in the conceptualization, leading the project, identifying the place, refurbishing, and the whole journey. It ignited a sense of leadership, righteousness, and management in me. This project helped underprivileged children, children of construction workers to have a chance at a decent and meaningful life. This directed my attention toward a purpose higher than self, watching them smile and be hopeful made me determined to help people.
My ‘digital quest’ project was a life-changing experience where I tied up with a foundation and worked on imparting and providing digital literacy where I facilitated and lead the process which was followed by a drive for digital waste. It made me understand real-life issues and hence do my part in contributing to problem-solving. It morphed me into a conscious citizen and hence see the bigger picture clearly and develop my independent opinions.
