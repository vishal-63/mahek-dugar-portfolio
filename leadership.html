Digital Quest

Empowering Community Through Digital Inclusion

Mission

To foster digital inclusion and sustainability by ensuring that individuals from all walks of life have equal access to digital skills, further enabling them to build a better tomorrow.

Journey at Digital Quest

Key Initiatives at Digital Quest

  • 1st Charity Badminton Tournament - 8th October 2022, O7 Club, Ahmedabad

The first step towards achieving our mission received an overwhelming response from 40 badminton enthusiasts(including some State Champions in the Open Category). The tournament witnessed some of the remarkable performances from the participants who have graciously contributed around $500 to our cause of promoting digital literacy.

  • 2nd Charity Badminton Tournament - 8th January 2023, Ahmedabad Youth Badminton Academy, Ahmedabad

Building on the success of the first tournament, we organized the second tournament exclusively for participants in the age group of 9 - 17. It was encouraging to have 75 young participants from across the State playing for the cause. We could raise another $700 from this tournament and also received inspiring feedback from participants and their parents.

  • E-Waste Drive- 16th April,2023, Gokuldham Society, Ahmedabad

Further towards our mission of digital sustainability, we did a week-long awareness campaign (through social media) emphasizing on what e-waste is and why it should be recycled. This was followed by organizing e-waste drives in the local communities and industrial areas. Approximately 100 kg of e-waste was collected which was sent to ECS, Environment Pvt. Ltd, a city based e-waste recycling company. Proceeds of around $200 from recycling the e-waste was added to the kitty of our mission.

The Outcome

First Computer Center at Nadiad in Collaboration with Tresna Foundation

Through our initiatives and fundraisers, we established 2 thriving computer centers in the Raghupura village at Nadiad, in collaboration with Tresna Foundation, Ahmedabad.
We donated 10 computer sets to the center where 50 students, mostly first-generation learners between the age of 13 - 16, are currently learning digital skills. 5 skilled trainers have been engaged to ensure a comprehensive learning experience.
The students' feedback at the center has been immensely rewarding. Their happiness and gratitude reinforce our belief in the importance of digital inclusion. We believe that equipping these students with digital skills will open better job opportunities for them, support their families, and contribute to the society at large.

Future Goal

Expanding Digital Quest's Reach

We aim to open more centers in future and also encourage senior citizens to join our programs, helping them become digitally and also financially literate. By expanding our reach and continuing our efforts, we envision a society where digital inclusion is the norm, opening doors to improved job opportunities and enhanced quality of life.

Math Club

As the co-founder of the Math Club at Sneh Srushti, an NGO run by Ahmedabad International School, I feel humbled to contribute to the educational journey of around 50 students from 7th to 9th standard. Engaging pedagogies and techniques were used to make complex concepts like rational and irrational numbers, GCD, LCM, and transposition easily understandable. For 7th and 8th graders, I also introduced interactive games that could facilitate learning of shapes, area, perimeter, and make learning math an enjoyable exercise. Being a part of this initiative has not only allowed me to share my passion for Mathematics but also enabled me to learn and grow alongside these bright minds. Witnessing the students' progress and enthusiasm has been a rewarding experience, and I look forward to nurturing their passion for mathematics further.
