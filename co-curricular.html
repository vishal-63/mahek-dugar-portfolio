Early Steps Academy Global Certification

During my time at Early Steps Academy from 2021 to 2023, I had the opportunity to explore diverse topics such as Global Awareness, Entrepreneurship, 21st-Century Skills, Finance, Global History, and Technology Miracles with a global cohort. The engaging pedagogy of analyzing case studies of real life companies like KFC, H&M, Netflix, Amazon, 23andMe helped me gain valuable insights into various business and technological advancements and also developed my critical thinking and communication skills.

Olympiads & MUNs

In my pursuit of knowledge and personal growth, I have enthusiastically participated in MUN’s various Olympiads. It all began with the Anand Niketan Model UN 3rd Edition, where I represented South Africa at the United Nations Office of Drugs and Crime in 2019.

Additionally, I achieved recognition by qualifying for the ASSET Talent Search as part of the top 5% of students who appeared for ASSET 2019. At the SOF-International English Olympiad in 2021, I secured the 25th Zonal Rank and was awarded the Medal of Distinction.

Most recently, at the Global Economics Olympiad 2022, I qualified for the Regional Round with an ‘A’ grade representing the Gujarat region. These experiences have been transformative, allowing me to explore new fields, test my knowledge, and strive for excellence.
