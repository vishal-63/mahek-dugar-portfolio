Virtual Equity Investing

The Wharton Global High School Investment Competition allowed me to explore my stint at equity investing. Out of 1,400 teams representing 53 countries, our school team could successfully advance up to the quarterfinals.

We explored equity investing on an online stock market simulator using $100,000 virtual cash. Based on the long term financial goals of the investor in the case study, as a team, we built a strategy considering risk, diversification, company and industry analysis. Analytical tools like CAGR, P/E and D/E ratios were used as we invested in the IT, Finance, Health and Energy sectors, wherein our portfolio saw around 7% return over the investing period of 4 months. This entire experience ignited my interests in the area of finance and investment decision making.
